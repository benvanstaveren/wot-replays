[TDC-O] Tidmadt Death Crew - Oceania Division

Death is our business and business has been GOOD!!

A little bit about [TDC]:
We are a Multinational Gaming Community, that currently has members based in Australia, New Zealand, United States, Canada, Japan, Malaysia, Singapore and Argentina. With the demographic that we have, TeamSpeak can be seen to be populated most times of the day, so there is always someone on to chat, and platoon up with.


Our primary goal is to have fun, gaming with like minded gamers. However, when it comes to clan wars we can be serious.


After being successful on the NA server, we have branched out and TDC-O is our Oceanic Division (SEA Server). With superior tankers, we fight quantity with quality.


If your looking to join [TDC], then head over to our website first. www.tidmadt.com and put an application in the site first.
